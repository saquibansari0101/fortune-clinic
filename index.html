Frequently Asked Questions

Your Queries, Our Answers

  • What is the recovery time after a hair transplant procedure?

    The recovery time can vary depending on the individual and the technique used. Generally, it takes about 7-10 days for the transplanted area to heal, and you can typically resume normal activities within a week.

  • Are hair transplant procedures permanent?

    Yes, hair transplant procedures are considered permanent. The transplanted hair follicles are taken from areas resistant to balding and are genetically programmed to continue growing even after transplantation.

  • Are there any non-surgical options for hair restoration?

    Yes, non-surgical options such as Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy and Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP) can help with hair restoration. PRP therapy involves injecting platelet-rich plasma derived from your blood into the scalp to promote hair growth.

  • What is the recommended age for considering a hair transplant?

    Hair transplant procedures can be performed on individuals who are experiencing significant hair loss or thinning. The ideal age for a hair transplant varies, but it is generally recommended for individuals over the age of 25 when the pattern of hair loss stabilizes.

  • Is laser hair removal suitable for all skin types?

    Laser hair removal can be performed on most skin types. However, the effectiveness may vary depending on the contrast between the hair color and the skin tone. Our professionals can evaluate your skin and hair type during the consultation to determine the most suitable treatment approach.
